The race I had no plans to do-The Life Changer

This race was the Spaceport Relay 24-hour race. We raced from El Paso, Texas, to the Spaceport, NM. A friend called me to let me know that The Ultimate Gift of Life was going to run this to advertise. Our goal was to have a 12-person team, but we only got 6, and the sixth one was last minute because someone couldn't do it. Staying up, not getting much sleep, not having a team (until David called me), and running more mileage than I ever ran before were scary concepts. This was one of the best things I have done in my entire life
